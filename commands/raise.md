# !raise

**Tags:** `player`

Sends the raise menu to the specified player.

**Usage**: `!raise (power) (player)`

**Parameters**:
- `power`: The strength of the raise to give
  - Options:
    - 1: Raise I
    - 2: Raise II
    - 3: Raise III
    - 4: Arise (Raise III & Reraise)
- `player`: The name of the player to send the raise
