# !addtempitem

**Tags:** `temporary`, `item`

Adds a temporary item to the specified player.

**Usage**: `!addtempitem <itemId> (quantity)`

**Parameters**:
- `itemID`: The ID of the temporary item to give to the player
- `quantity`: (Optional) The amount of temporary items to give
